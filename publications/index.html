HumanFlow: Controllable Human Image Generation via Flow Matching

Wenzhuo Fan, Hongsheng Zheng

SIGGRAPH 2026

HumanFlow is a unified flow-matching framework for controllable full-body human image generation. It introduces a Control Encoder, Token-ControlNet, and topology-aware loss for structural consistency, along with the large-scale MiCoGen dataset, achieving improved controllability and fidelity over existing diffusion and flow-based methods.
